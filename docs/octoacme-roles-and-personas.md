# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## QA/Testing Lead

### Role Summary
QA/Testing Leads own quality assurance strategy, testing workflows, and acceptance validation. They partner with developers and product/project leads to define quality standards throughout delivery.

### Responsibilities
- Define test strategy and quality gates for each release
- Create and maintain test plans aligned with acceptance criteria
- Lead manual and automated testing efforts
- Validate acceptance criteria before stories are marked done
- Identify and triage defects with developers
- Advocate for quality and testability improvements

### Goals
- Ensure high-quality deliverables that meet acceptance criteria
- Reduce regression and production defects
- Improve test coverage and automation

### Typical Communication
- Sprint planning and backlog refinement sessions
- Daily standups and test result summaries
- Release sign-off and smoke test verification

---

## Technical Architect

### Role Summary
Technical Architects guide architectural decisions, technical strategy, and design reviews. They ensure solutions are scalable, maintainable, and aligned with long-term technical vision.

### Responsibilities
- Review architectural designs and technical approaches
- Advise on scalability, performance, and technical debt trade-offs
- Document key technical decisions and architectural patterns
- Mentor developers on best practices and design patterns
- Identify technical risks and propose mitigation strategies
- Support cross-team integration and API design

### Goals
- Ensure robust, scalable, and maintainable technical solutions
- Reduce technical debt and rework
- Enable long-term product sustainability

### Typical Communication
- Design review meetings and architecture forums
- Technical documentation and decision logs
- Code review feedback on complex components

---

## Scrum Master / Agile Coach

### Role Summary
Scrum Masters / Agile Coaches facilitate agile processes, remove blockers, and help teams continuously improve workflow and collaboration.

### Responsibilities
- Facilitate sprint planning, standups, reviews, and retrospectives
- Identify and remove process blockers
- Coach teams on agile principles and practices
- Maintain sprint health metrics and team velocity trends
- Help resolve interpersonal or team dynamics issues
- Drive continuous process improvement initiatives

### Goals
- Enable high-performing, self-organizing teams
- Optimize delivery cadence and predictability
- Foster psychological safety and continuous learning

### Typical Communication
- Sprint ceremonies and retrospective facilitation
- One-on-one coaching conversations
- Process health dashboards and sprint metrics

---

## Business Analyst

### Role Summary
Business Analysts bridge product vision and technical delivery by gathering requirements, clarifying business needs, and ensuring solutions align with business objectives.

### Responsibilities
- Gather and document business requirements with stakeholders
- Analyze and clarify ambiguous requirements
- Create requirement specifications and use cases
- Support acceptance criteria definition and user story writing
- Validate solutions against business requirements
- Communicate business context to technical teams

### Goals
- Ensure delivered solutions address real business needs
- Reduce rework due to misaligned requirements
- Improve stakeholder satisfaction and adoption

### Typical Communication
- Stakeholder interviews and requirements workshops
- Requirement documentation and specification reviews
- Backlog refinement and acceptance criteria reviews

---

## Security & Compliance Officer

### Role Summary
Security & Compliance Officers ensure projects meet security, privacy, and regulatory requirements. They partner with delivery teams to build security into the lifecycle.

### Responsibilities
- Define security and compliance requirements for projects
- Perform security design reviews and threat modeling
- Oversee security testing and vulnerability scanning
- Ensure compliance with privacy and regulatory standards
- Manage security incident response and escalation
- Provide security guidance and training to teams

### Goals
- Build secure, compliant products that protect user data
- Reduce security vulnerabilities and compliance risk
- Maintain stakeholder trust and regulatory standing

### Typical Communication
- Security design reviews and threat modeling sessions
- Security testing and scan result reviews
- Compliance audits and incident response coordination
